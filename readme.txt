=== Website Callback ===
Tags: callback, voip
Requires at least: 2.7
Tested up to: 3.1
Stable tag: trunk

Allow potential customers to initiate a call direct from your office to them on the web.

== Description ==

This plugin uses the Netfuse service to initiate a phone call. First you will need a Netfuse account! You can get this by emailing sales@netfuse.org.

== Installation ==

###Configure Plugin###

Go to the settings page, add your Netfuse account details and your company number.

###Insert The Plugin###

Use the [callback] shortcode (once) anywhere in your post :)
